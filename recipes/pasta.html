Skillet chicken pasta recipe

Description

  • Skillet Chicken Pasta is a delicious, one-pan dish made with juicy chicken and pasta in a creamy sauce.
  • This recipe combines tender chicken with garlic, spinach, and sun-dried tomatoes, all cooked in one skillet.
  • It’s a quick and easy dinner option that is both flavorful and satisfying.
  • The creamy sauce is made with a blend of cream, Parmesan, and herbs, making it rich and indulgent.
  • This dish pairs perfectly with a side of garlic bread or a simple salad.

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 200g pasta (penne, fusilli, or spaghetti)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/2 cup sun-dried tomatoes, chopped
  • 2 cups fresh spinach
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup chicken broth
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the chicken breasts and cook until golden brown on both sides and cooked through, about 6-7 minutes per side. Remove the chicken from the skillet and set aside.
  4. In the same skillet, add the garlic and sauté for 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Add the sun-dried tomatoes and spinach to the skillet. Cook until the spinach wilts, about 2-3 minutes.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream and chicken broth, stirring to combine. Bring to a simmer and cook for 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the skillet, tossing to coat in the sauce. Add the Parmesan cheese and stir until melted and creamy.
  8. Slice the cooked chicken and add it to the skillet with the pasta. Toss everything together and heat through.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste, then garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
