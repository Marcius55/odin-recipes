American goulash

Description

  • American Goulash is a comforting one-pot dish made with ground beef, pasta, and a savory tomato sauce.
  • It’s a quick and easy meal, often referred to as "beefaroni" or "American chop suey."
  • The dish is a perfect family dinner, rich in flavor and made with simple ingredients.
  • Seasoned with garlic, onions, and Italian herbs, it’s both hearty and satisfying.
  • Serve with a side of garlic bread or a green salad for a complete meal.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) ground beef
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 (15 oz) can tomato sauce
  • 1 (14.5 oz) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (6 oz) can tomato paste
  • 2 cups elbow macaroni (or any pasta of choice)
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese (optional, for topping)
  • 2 cups water or beef broth

Steps

  1. In a large skillet or pot, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic to the skillet. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onion becomes translucent.
  3. Stir in the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  4. Add 2 cups of water or beef broth to the skillet, then bring the mixture to a simmer.
  5. Add the elbow macaroni to the skillet and stir to combine. Cover and simmer for 15-20 minutes, or until the pasta is cooked and tender, stirring occasionally. Add more liquid if necessary during cooking.
  6. Once the pasta is cooked and the sauce has thickened, remove from heat.
  7. If desired, sprinkle shredded cheddar cheese over the top and cover until the cheese melts.
  8. Serve hot, garnished with additional cheese or herbs if preferred.
